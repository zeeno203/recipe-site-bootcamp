Pao Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 2 dried chilies
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Preparation Time

30 minutes

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces and marinate with soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sugar.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and stir-fry garlic and chilies.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Toss in peanuts and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice.
