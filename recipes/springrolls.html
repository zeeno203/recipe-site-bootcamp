Spring Rolls

Ingredients

  • 200g spring roll wrappers
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Oil for frying

Preparation Time

25 minutes

Instructions

  1. Mix cabbage, carrots, and bean sprouts with soy sauce.
  2. Place a small amount of the mixture in a wrapper and roll tightly.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry rolls until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
