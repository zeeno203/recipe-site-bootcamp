Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation Time

20 minutes

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.
  4. Combine spaghetti, pancetta, and egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then serve!
