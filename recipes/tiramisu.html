Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 200g ladyfingers
  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 2 cups coffee
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g sugar
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Preparation Time

30 minutes + chilling time

Instructions

  1. Brew strong coffee and let it cool.
  2. Separate egg yolks and whites. Beat yolks with sugar until creamy, then add mascarpone.
  3. Whisk egg whites until stiff and fold into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers into coffee and layer them in a dish.
  5. Spread mascarpone mixture over the layer and repeat.
  6. Dust with cocoa powder and chill for at least 4 hours.
